About Ban.Q

In Kenya and Africa, money is a scarce resource for middle-to-low-income earners. In fact, getting it is difficult, as if keeping it is not hard enough. The means to grow it are skewed to the disadvantage of the money owner.

On the other hand, getting a loan is no longer simple. Many offers but also too many traps, high costs, too invasive and so much more. There is lacking a one-stop shop or service to offer knowledge on available savings, loans and investment solutions, compare, and select what best suits their needs.

Mobile phones are becoming a ubiquitous companion of many disenfranchised people. Leveraging on this, BanQ is Marketplace where people state their financial needs and financial institutions respond to those needs by offering people the best rates and deals.
