Get started





1  Download ban.q app


Go to playstore, download ban.q app and install it on your phone.






2 Make a deposit request


You have some money and you don't know which bank to deposit with to get maximum returns? Place a deposit request for banks to give you offers to choose from!










3   Accept best bid


Choose the best offer for your deposit request. Once you are satisfied with the offer accept the offer.









4  Compare cost of various banking services.


If you don't know the best bank to get a service, worry no more. In case of two banks offering the same service, you can compare the cost of the services and decide which bank should serve you!









5  Loan Calculator


Use our loan calculator to help you decide which loan provider is has terms that suit you best.









6  Bank Branch Finder


You are in a new town and don't know the location of your banks branch? Use Banq branch finder for directions.
